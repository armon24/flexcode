Welcome to MentorMatch!

Thank you for signing up to be a Mentee. Here's what you need to know:

We have paired you up with a mentor to advise you with a focus on what focus you listed as the area you needed advice in. Your match is under the match tab and you will see information about them including their major, minor, etc. You are also provided their email address if you want to contact them to set up a meeting. We hope you enjoy this service and get all your questions answered. Remember your mentor is here to help and signed up to answer your questions. Don't be nervous or afraid to ask anything. That's what we're here for!
