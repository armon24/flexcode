Welcome to MentorMatch!

Thank you so much for being a Mentor. Here's what you need to know:

Under the My Match tab you will find your mentee as well as some information about them. This includes their academic and social information as well as the type of advice they're primarily looking for. You also have their email and some favorite locations. If they haven't already emailed you go ahead and send them an email introducing yourself and suggest a time to meet up. Their favorite locations should be listed along with their other personal information. You will get one match who is matched with you based on information you filled out upon registering.
